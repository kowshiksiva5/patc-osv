Validation path

Pilot without control risk.

  1. Offline corridor modelUse existing observations plus new field data to calibrate a selected Bengaluru sector.
  2. Shadow-mode replayRun PATC recommendations against fixed-time and actuated baselines without touching live control.
  3. Operator workflowExpose the reason, confidence, expected effect, and fallback condition for each recommendation.
  4. Controlled pilot packagePrepare safety review, mock-controller demo, deployment checklist, and authority-ready memo.

Company

Built in Bengaluru for mixed-traffic cities.

PATC came from six months of repeated Bengaluru traffic pain, wrong early ideas, manual observations, commuter conversations, and failed one-signal thinking. The current thesis is corridor coordination first: model the flow, recommend in shadow mode, and earn trust before control.
