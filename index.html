Validation path

Earn trust in shadow mode before control.

  1. Offline sector modelSelect one Bengaluru sector with 4-6 connected signals and define baseline timing metrics.
  2. Field evidenceCollect 120-180 observation hours across peak, off-peak, rain, weekend, school, office, and seasonal variation.
  3. Shadow replayRun PATC recommendations against fixed-time and actuated baselines without touching live control.
  4. Pilot packagePrepare dashboard, mock-controller demo, safety/fallback playbook, validation report, and authority-ready memo.

Company

Built from Bengaluru traffic, not slideware.

PATC started with six months of repeated Bengaluru traffic pain: watching one signal clear while the next road stayed blocked, testing wrong one-signal ideas, taking manual observations, talking to commuters, and turning the problem into a corridor-flow model.

The company is focused on a narrow first wedge: shadow-mode recommendations for mixed-traffic corridors, with enough evidence, simulation depth, and operator review to earn trust before touching live control.
